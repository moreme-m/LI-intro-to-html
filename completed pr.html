SUGAR GLIDERS

Sugar gliders are squirrel-sized arboreal marsupials that inhabit the forests of Australia and New Guinea. Sugar gliders have opposable thumbs and four fingers on both hands and feet. Each finger has a sharp toe that can hook like Velcro to most non-slick surfaces.
